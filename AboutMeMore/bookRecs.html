Fablehaven by Brandon Mull

This series is about a girl and her brother who go to their grandparents estate and find out it's a safehaven for magical creatures. I first read this series in middle school and it's been a comfort series ever since. Still a really fun read with twists that you would never expect.

Alice in Zombieland by Gena Showalter

This series is about a girl in the wake of loosing her family, who can now see zombies. She has to battle a new school and zombies who are now out to get her. It's an action-adventure romance set in high school, what more could you want?

Strange Angels by Lili St. Crow

This series is about a girl who hunts the things that go bump in the night with her father. But what happens when her father comes back from a job... as a zombie?! Uh oh, it's a love triangle, but the monster lore is actually really good too.
