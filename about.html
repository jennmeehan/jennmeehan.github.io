Hi there, my name is Jennifer Meehan. I am a fourth year Creative Technology and Design student at CU Boulder. I started my college career in Aerospace Engineering but felt that it was lacking in creativity. I am a very creative person and I have finally found my home in this new major. I am from Pueblo, Colorado but I have family ties in New England. I am a self proclaimed observer of humanity and I am an ally in every sense of the word.
